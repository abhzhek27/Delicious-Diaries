CHICKEN 65
01 INTRO 02 SPICES REQUIRED 03 START COOKING

CRISPY HEAT IN EVERY BITE

Born in the heart of South India, Chicken 65 is the fiery, crunchy, and irresistible snack that won hearts across the nation. Served hot and full of flavor, it’s the perfect mix of crispiness, spice, and street-style satisfaction.

THE SPICES

2 tsp red chili powder, 1 tsp turmeric, 1 tsp black pepper, 1 tsp cumin powder, 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste, 1 tsp garam masala, 1/2 tsp coriander powder, 1 tbsp lemon juice, salt to taste, 1 egg, 2 tbsp corn flour, 1 tbsp rice flour, curry leaves, and green chilies for garnishing.

Cooking Steps

Step 1

Cut boneless chicken into small bite-sized pieces. Marinate with ginger-garlic paste, chili powder, turmeric, salt, lemon juice, and egg. Let it rest for 1 hour.

Step 2

Just before frying, mix in corn flour and rice flour to give it that crisp texture.

Step 3

Heat oil and deep-fry the chicken pieces on medium flame until golden and crispy. Remove and drain on paper towels.

Step 4

In a separate pan, sauté curry leaves and slit green chilies in little oil. Toss the fried chicken pieces in this for extra flavor.

Step 5

Serve hot with lemon wedges and onion rings. Enjoy as a starter or snack!
