LIME JUICE
01 INTRO 02 INGREDIENTS REQUIRED 03 START COOKING

Zest up your life — one drop of lime at a time!

Lime juice originates from the lime fruit, which comes from the Citrus aurantiifolia (Key lime) and Citrus latifolia (Persian lime) trees

INGREDIENTS REQUIRED

Fresh lime juice 2 to 3 tablespoons (from 1–2 limes)
Water – 1 cup (cold or chilled)
Sugar or honey – 1 to 2 teaspoons (adjust to taste)
Salt – a pinch (optional, enhances flavor)
Ice cubes – optional, for serving

Cooking Steps

Step 1

Cut fresh limes and squeeze out the juice into a bowl or glass

Step 2

Add 1 cup of cold water to the lime juice.

Step 3

Stir in sugar (or honey) and a pinch of salt. Mix well until dissolved.

Step 4

Add ice cubes and optionally garnish with mint leaves. Serve immediately!
