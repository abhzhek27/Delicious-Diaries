WATERMELON JUICE
01 INTRO 02 INGREDIENTS REQUIRED 03 START BLENDING

Refresh your soul — sip the sweetness of summer!

Watermelon juice is a hydrating, naturally sweet drink made from fresh watermelon. It's packed with electrolytes, antioxidants, and summer vibes.

INGREDIENTS REQUIRED

Fresh watermelon – 2 cups, diced and deseeded
Lemon juice – 1 tablespoon (optional, adds zing)
Mint leaves – a few (optional, for freshness)
Sugar or honey – 1 teaspoon (optional, if needed)
Ice cubes – as needed
Water – 1/4 cup (only if needed to adjust consistency)

Blending Steps

Step 1

Cut watermelon into small cubes and remove seeds.

Step 2

Add watermelon cubes to a blender. Optionally add lemon juice, mint, and sugar/honey.

Step 3

Blend until smooth. Add a little water if it's too thick.

Step 4

Strain (optional), pour into glasses over ice, garnish with mint, and serve chilled!
