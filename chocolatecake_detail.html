CHOCOLATE CAKE
01 OVERVIEW 02 INGREDIENTS 03 BAKE & FINISH

A SLICE OF HEAVEN

This chocolate cake is the perfect balance of rich cocoa flavor, moist crumb, and creamy frosting. Whether it’s for birthdays, celebrations, or sweet cravings — it’s always the right time for chocolate cake.

THE INGREDIENT

1½ cups all-purpose flour, 1½ cups sugar, ¾ cup unsweetened cocoa powder, 1½ tsp baking powder, 1½ tsp baking soda, 2 eggs, 1 cup milk, ½ cup vegetable oil, 2 tsp vanilla extract, 1 cup boiling water, and a pinch of salt.

Baking Steps

Step 1

Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F). Grease and line two 8-inch round cake pans.

Step 2

In a large bowl, mix flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.

Step 3

Add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla to the dry ingredients. Beat until smooth using a whisk or electric mixer.

Step 4

Stir in boiling water gradually. The batter will be thin — this keeps the cake moist.

Step 5

Pour batter evenly into prepared pans. Bake for 30–35 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean. Cool before frosting.

Optional Frosting

Top with chocolate ganache, buttercream, or whipped cream and decorate with sprinkles, fruit, or chocolate chips.
