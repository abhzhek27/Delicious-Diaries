Sip the Sweetness of Nature

Grape juice is a delicious and nutritious drink made from crushed and blended grapes. Known for its vibrant color and antioxidants, it's both refreshing and heart-healthy.

INGREDIENTS REQUIRED

Fresh grapes – 2 cups (seedless, black or green)
Water – 1/2 cup (optional for blending)
Sugar or honey – to taste (optional)
Lemon juice – 1 tsp (optional for tang)
Ice cubes – for serving
Mint – for garnish (optional)

Juicing Steps

Step 1

Rinse the grapes thoroughly and remove them from the stems.

Step 2

Blend the grapes with optional water until smooth. Strain to remove pulp and skin.

Step 3

Add lemon juice and sweetener if desired. Mix well.

Step 4

Serve chilled over ice, garnished with mint leaves. Enjoy your fresh grape juice!
