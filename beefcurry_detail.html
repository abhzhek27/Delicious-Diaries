BEEF CURRY
01 INTRO 02 SPICES REQUIRED 03 START COOKING

HEARTY & HOMELY

Beef Curry is a bold and comforting dish, slow-cooked in richly spiced gravy. From festive meals to everyday cravings, its deep flavor and tender texture bring warmth and tradition to every table it touches.

THE SPICES

2 tsp red chili powder, 1 tsp turmeric, 1.5 tsp coriander powder, 1 tsp garam masala, 1 tsp black pepper, 1 tsp fennel powder, 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste, 2 bay leaves, 2 cloves, 1 cinnamon stick, 2 green cardamoms, 1 tsp mustard seeds, 1 sprig curry leaves, coconut oil, salt to taste.

Cooking Steps

Step 1

Marinate beef with turmeric, chili powder, coriander, ginger-garlic paste, and salt. Let it rest for 30 minutes to 1 hour.

Step 2

Heat coconut oil in a pressure cooker. Add mustard seeds, whole spices, and curry leaves. Sauté till aromatic.

Step 3

Add sliced onions and cook until golden brown. Add the marinated beef and sauté on high heat for 5–7 minutes.

Step 4

Add water just enough to cover the meat. Pressure cook for 4–5 whistles or until the beef is tender.

Step 5

Open lid, add garam masala and simmer uncovered until gravy thickens. Garnish with curry leaves or coriander. Serve hot with rice, appam, or parotta.
