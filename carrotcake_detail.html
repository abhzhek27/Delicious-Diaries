CARROT CAKE
01 OVERVIEW 02 INGREDIENTS 03 BAKE & FINISH

A CLASSIC, MOIST & SPICED DELIGHT

Carrot Cake is a moist, warmly spiced cake with grated carrots adding natural sweetness and texture. Topped with smooth cream cheese frosting, it’s a beloved treat perfect for celebrations or everyday indulgence.

THE INGREDIENT

2 cups all-purpose flour, 2 tsp baking powder, 1½ tsp baking soda, 1½ tsp ground cinnamon, ½ tsp ground nutmeg, ½ tsp salt, 1¼ cups vegetable oil, 1 cup brown sugar, 1 cup granulated sugar, 4 large eggs, 3 cups grated carrots, 1 cup chopped walnuts (optional), 1 tsp vanilla extract, and cream cheese frosting (optional).

Baking Steps

Step 1

Preheat oven to 175°C (350°F). Grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans or line with parchment paper.

Step 2

In a medium bowl, whisk together flour, baking powder, baking soda, cinnamon, nutmeg, and salt.

Step 3

In a large bowl, beat oil, brown sugar, and granulated sugar until well combined. Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in vanilla extract.

Step 4

Gradually add dry ingredients to the wet mixture, mixing until just combined. Fold in grated carrots and walnuts if using.

Step 5

Divide batter evenly into prepared pans. Bake for 35-40 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean. Let cool completely.

Step 6

Frost with cream cheese frosting if desired. Chill briefly before serving. Enjoy your moist and flavorful Carrot Cake!
