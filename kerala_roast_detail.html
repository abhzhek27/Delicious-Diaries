A FIERY LEGACY

Bold, spicy, and deeply aromatic — Kerala Chicken Roast is a dish that reflects the vibrant heart of South Indian cuisine. Traditionally cooked in coconut oil and enriched with the warmth of curry leaves and spices, this roast captures the soul of Kerala’s culinary heritage, bringing families together one delicious bite at a time.

THE SPICES

1 tsp turmeric powder, 2 tsp red chili powder, 1 tsp black pepper, 1 tsp garam masala, 1 tsp coriander powder, 1/2 tsp fennel powder, 1 tsp crushed ginger-garlic paste, 1/2 tsp mustard seeds, fresh curry leaves, a pinch of fenugreek, and salt to taste. All roasted in rich coconut oil for that authentic Kerala flavor

Cooking Steps

Step 1

Marinate chicken with turmeric, chili powder, vinegar, and salt. Let it sit for 1 hour

Step 2

Sauté sliced onions, garlic, ginger, green chilies in coconut oil till golden brown.

Step 3

Add marinated chicken and roast on medium heat till well coated and cooked.

Step 4

Add curry leaves, black pepper, garam masala and continue roasting until dry and brown.
