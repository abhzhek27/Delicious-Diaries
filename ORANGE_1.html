ORANGE JUICE

Freshness in Every Sip – Taste the Sunshine!

Orange juice, extracted from ripe oranges, is a classic beverage enjoyed worldwide for its tangy sweetness and rich Vitamin C content. Whether freshly squeezed or chilled, it brightens your day!

INGREDIENTS REQUIRED

Fresh oranges – 4 to 6 medium-sized
Water – optional (for dilution)
Sugar or honey – to taste (optional)
Ice cubes – for serving
Mint leaves – optional for garnish

Juicing Steps

Step 1

Wash and cut the oranges in half. Remove seeds if needed.

Step 2

Use a manual or electric juicer to extract the juice from each half.

Step 3

Strain the juice to remove pulp (optional), and stir in sweetener if desired.

Step 4

Serve with ice cubes and garnish with mint. Enjoy chilled!
