VANILLA CAKE
01 OVERVIEW 02 INGREDIENTS 03 BAKE & FINISH

THE ESSENCE OF SIMPLICITY

2½ cups all-purpose flour, 2½ tsp baking powder, ½ tsp salt, ¾ cup unsalted butter (softened), 1¾ cups sugar, 4 eggs, 1 tbsp vanilla extract, 1 cup milk, and optional: whipped cream or frosting for topping.

THE INGREDIENT

1½ cups all-purpose flour, 1½ cups sugar, ¾ cup unsweetened cocoa powder, 1½ tsp baking powder, 1½ tsp baking soda, 2 eggs, 1 cup milk, ½ cup vegetable oil, 2 tsp vanilla extract, 1 cup boiling water, and a pinch of salt.

Baking Steps

Step 1

Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F). Grease and line two 8-inch cake pans with parchment paper.

Step 2

In a bowl, whisk together flour, baking powder, and salt. Set aside.

Step 3

In another bowl, cream butter and sugar until light and fluffy. Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition.

Step 4

Mix in vanilla extract. Gradually add dry ingredients alternating with milk. Beat until smooth.

Step 5

Divide the batter evenly between the pans. Bake for 30–35 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.

Step 6

Cool completely before frosting or serving. Enjoy with cream, fruit, or just as it is!
